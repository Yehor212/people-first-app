Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-10

Summary

  • Your data is stored on your device by default.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • If you enable cloud sync, your data is stored in your account.

Data we store

ZenFlow stores your wellness entries (mood, habits, focus sessions, gratitude) on your device. This data stays local unless you choose to sign in and enable cloud sync.

Cloud sync

If you sign in and use cloud sync, your data is stored in your account on our backend. This is used only to sync your data between devices.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications yet.

Analytics

Analytics are optional and can be disabled in Settings.
