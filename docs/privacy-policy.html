3. How Your Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No internet connection is required to use the app
  • Clearing browser/app data will delete your information
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device lock (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)
  • No data transmission means no risk of server breaches

  • Cloud sync is completely optional - the app works fully offline
